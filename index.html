Meet Dr. Sayan Bhowmik

About

Dr. Sayan Bhowmik is a clinician with a disciplined medical foundation and a growing interest in the intersection of medicine, technology, and evidence-driven healthcare. With over four years of hands-on clinical experience and more than 2,000 patients managed across varied settings, he brings a balanced mix of practical medical judgment and analytical thinking.

Education & Training

Dr. Bhowmik earned his MBBS degree from a Government Medical College with academic distinction, followed by residency training in the Orthopedic Department of a major Government Medical College Hospital. His training exposed him to high-volume clinical environments, trauma care, perioperative management, and interdisciplinary medical decision-making—developing both speed and accuracy in diagnosis.

Commitment to Care

He prioritizes clear, transparent communication and develops practical, evidence-based, personalized treatment plans. Known for accurate diagnosis and strong follow-up, he ensures patients feel informed and confident throughout their care journey.

Professional Interests

Beyond conventional clinical work, he actively explores emerging medical technologies, AI in healthcare, data-driven decision support, and the evolving future of patient care models. This dual perspective—clinical grounding + modern medical innovation—shapes his long-term professional mission.

Treatments

Comprehensive evaluation and evidence-based treatment for common and chronic health conditions. Care plans are personalised to each patient with a focus on long-term wellness.

Hypertension

Management includes blood pressure profiling, cardiovascular risk evaluation, kidney assessment, ECG interpretation, personalised medication selection, and structured lifestyle planning to prevent heart, kidney, and stroke-related complications.

Diabetes

Diabetes care includes blood sugar profiling, HbA1c interpretation, oral/insulin therapy planning, diet and lifestyle counselling, and screening for complications involving the eyes, kidneys, nerves, and feet.

Heart Attack & Stroke Management

Evaluation of chest pain, breathlessness, palpitations, and stroke symptoms using ECG, vitals, and risk profiling. Includes emergency guidance, preventive therapy, cholesterol management, and long-term cardiac protection strategies.

Back Pain & Knee Pain

Detailed assessment of posture, muscle strain, nerve involvement, and arthritis. Management includes pain control, physiotherapy guidance, ergonomic corrections, strengthening plans, and long-term prevention of recurrence.

Asthma & Lung Diseases

Comprehensive care for Asthma, COPD, and Bronchitis including lung evaluation, oxygen assessment, inhaler optimisation, allergy control, and personalised treatment plans to reduce flare-ups and improve breathing.

Cough–Cold–Flu

Structured evaluation of throat, sinuses, and chest, followed by targeted medication, hydration planning, fever monitoring, and prevention of complications such as sinusitis, bronchitis, or pneumonia.
